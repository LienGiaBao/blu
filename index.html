Overview.

With the desire to promote mutual understanding between the Vietnamese students and friends around the world, as a contribution to global peace, we have initiated “Cultural Exchange Project” aimed at spreading and sharing the beautiful image of the nature and people of Bac Lieu province and Vietnam country to foreign students.

This project is non-profit and does not intend to harm any agency, organization, or individual. This project is not directly under the management of any state agency, political or social organization, but is subject to the legal management of competent authorities.

Places.

Bac Lieu Prince's House

Timeless Majestic Architecture

Located at 13 Dien Bien Phu Street, Ward 13, in the heart of Bac Lieu City, the Bac Lieu Prince's House stands out with its grandeur and timeless Western architectural style. It's also a cultural site associated with many stories and legends about the life of the most prominent young master in the Western region in the 1919s. Link.
